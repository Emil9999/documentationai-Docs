## Domain Model Overview

The Backend API manages the relationship between employers (Companies), their job postings (Jobs), where those jobs are published (Talent Channels & Job Bookings), and the candidates who apply (Applications).

```
[Company] ─── (has) ───► [Job]
   │                       │
   │                    (booked as)
   │                       │
   ▼                       ▼
[Campaign] ◄── (groups) ── [Job Booking] ───► [Talent Channel]
                               │
                           (receives)
                               │
                               ▼
                          [Application]
```

## Core Entities

### Companies
A **Company** represents an employer or client on the platform. It holds billing details, feature configurations, and active integrations.
- **Key Fields**:
  - `id` (Integer): Unique identifier.
  - `name` (String): Name of the company.
  - `is_active` (Boolean): Whether the company is active.
  - `auth_company_id` (String): PropelAuth organization ID.
  - `fallback_job` (ForeignKey): Default job to map applications to when no specific job is matched.
- **Endpoints**: `/api/companies/`

### Jobs
A **Job** is a specific job posting created by a company. It contains the job title, description, requirements, location, and optional screening questions.
- **Key Fields**:
  - `id` (Integer): Unique identifier.
  - `title` (String): Job title.
  - `description` (String): Rich text job description.
  - `location` (String): Job location.
  - `company` (ForeignKey): The company that owns this job.
  - `screening_questions` (JSON): Custom questions for candidates.
- **Endpoints**: `/api/jobs/`

### Talent Channels
A **Talent Channel** is an external job board or platform where jobs can be published (e.g., StepStone, Indeed, Kleinanzeigen, Perspective, Facebook).
- **Key Fields**:
  - `id` (Integer): Unique identifier.
  - `name` (String): Display name of the channel.
  - `slug` (String): Unique URL-friendly identifier (e.g., `indeed`, `stepstone`, `perspective`).
- **Endpoints**: `/api/talent_channels/`

### Job Bookings
A **Job Booking** represents the publication of a specific Job on a specific Talent Channel. It tracks the status of the posting, publication dates, and external identifiers.
- **Key Fields**:
  - `id` (Integer): Unique identifier.
  - `job` (ForeignKey): The job being published.
  - `talent_channel` (ForeignKey): The target channel.
  - `status` (String): Publication status (e.g., `active`, `paused`, `completed`).
  - `external_job_id` (String): Identifier used by the external channel.
- **Endpoints**: `/api/job_bookings/`

### Campaigns
A **Campaign** groups multiple Job Bookings together for promotional purposes. This allows companies to manage budgets, track performance, and freeze/unfreeze bookings within a campaign.
- **Key Fields**:
  - `id` (Integer): Unique identifier.
  - `name` (String): Campaign name.
  - `company` (ForeignKey): The company running the campaign.
  - `is_active` (Boolean): Whether the campaign is currently running.
- **Endpoints**: `/api/campaigns/`

### Applications
An **Application** represents a candidate's submission for a job posting. It contains candidate details, resumes, answers to screening questions, and tracking status.
- **Key Fields**:
  - `id` (Integer): Unique identifier.
  - `job` (ForeignKey): The job applied for.
  - `candidate_name` (String): Full name of the candidate.
  - `candidate_email` (String): Email address of the candidate.
  - `candidate_phone` (String): Phone number of the candidate.
  - `status` (String): Application status (e.g., `new`, `reviewed`, `hired`, `rejected`).
  - `screening_questions` (JSON): Candidate's answers to screening questions.
  - `attachments` (JSON): List of uploaded files (e.g., CV, cover letter).
- **Endpoints**: `/api/applications/`

### Magic Links
A **Magic Link** is a short-lived, token-based authentication link that grants passwordless access to specific company or application resources.
- **Key Fields**:
  - `token` (String): Unique secure token.
  - `company` (ForeignKey): The company associated with this link.
  - `expires_at` (DateTime): Expiration timestamp.
  - `is_used` (Boolean): Whether the link has already been used.
- **Endpoints**: `/api/magic/<token>/`

## Webhook Integrations

The Backend API features specialized webhook handlers to ingest candidate applications directly from external platforms.

### Indeed Apply Webhook
- **Endpoint**: `/api/webhooks/indeed/<job_id>/` or `/api/webhooks/indeed/<partner_id>/<company_id>/`
- **Behavior**: Parses Indeed's standard JSON payload, extracts candidate contact info, base64-encoded resume attachments, and screening question answers, then creates a local `Application` record.

### StepStone Apply Webhook
- **Endpoint**: `/api/webhooks/stepstone/<company_id>/apply/`
- **Behavior**: Receives applications from StepStone. It parses the `EffectiveUrl` (ATS URL) to extract the `external_job_id` (e.g., Personio job ID) and the `OriginalUrl` to extract the `agency_job_id` (e.g., Relaxx PID).
- **Inquiry Endpoint**: `/api/webhooks/stepstone/<company_id>/inquiry/` is used by StepStone to verify if a job is still active.

### Perspective Webhook
- **Endpoint**: `/api/webhooks/perspective/<company_id>/`
- **Behavior**: Receives lead data from Perspective funnels. It extracts the `external_job_id` from the bracketed text in the result page title (e.g., `[job_id]`) or from `utm_term`. If neither is present, it falls back to the company's `fallback_job`.
