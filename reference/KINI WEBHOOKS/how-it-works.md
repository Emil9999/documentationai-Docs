### [Application Sync Status Update](./sync-status-webhook)

Triggered when an application has been synchronized to the company's ATS. Useful for tracking the real-time status of candidate applications.

### [Job Updates](./job-webhook)

#### Job Created or Updated

Triggered when a new job is created or an existing job is modified. This includes changes to job titles, descriptions, or any other fields.

#### Job Archived

Triggered when a job is removed from the ATS. Keep your system in sync by removing outdated job entries.
