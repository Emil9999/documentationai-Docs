## Overview

Heyflow lead-generation funnels can be connected to your ATS through the Kini QuickApply integration. Candidate submissions in Heyflow are forwarded to your ATS in real time via a webhook.

## Webhook URL

In your Heyflow flow, go to the **Integrations** step and add a webhook action with the following URL:

```
https://api.getkini.com/webhooks/heyflow/{kini_agency_id}/{kini_company_id}/{external_job_id}
```

- **Agency ID**: your unique identifier, provided by the Kini team.
- **Company ID**: the company in Kini that the application should be forwarded to.
- **External Job ID**: the job's ID in the ATS.

Example: `https://api.getkini.com/webhooks/heyflow/12/345/xyz-67890`

## Mapping Fields

Heyflow's built-in form fields are automatically mapped to candidate fields in your ATS:

| Heyflow field | Candidate field |
|---|---|
| `first_name` | First name |
| `last_name` | Last name |
| `email` | Email |
| `phone` | Phone |
| `city` | City |
| `country` | Country |

Custom fields in Heyflow are forwarded as candidate properties and stored as comments on the application in the ATS.
